# Petmatch
Welcome to Petmatch, your go-to platform for finding the perfect pet! Whether you're looking to adopt a new furry friend or need to find a loving home for a pet, Petmatch makes the process easy and seamless.

## Overview
Petmatch connects pet owners who need to rehome their pets with potential adopters. Our platform allows pet owners to post detailed profiles of their pets, and adopters can browse through these profiles to find their ideal match. With Petmatch, finding a pet has never been easier.

## Features
Pet Profiles: Owners can create comprehensive profiles for their pets, including images, name, location, age, breed, health information, and a detailed description.
Browse Pets: Adopters can browse through the available pets using various filters to find the perfect companion.
Apply for Adoption: Adopters can apply for pets they are interested in. Owners will then review all received applications.
Owner Reviews: Pet owners can review applications and select the best adopter for their pet.
